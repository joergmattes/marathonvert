A family home in the heart of "La Bresse" is the ideal setting for a tango weekend. The weekend will take place on the 27th, 28th and 29st of July plus one additional day, Monday 30th of july. As most participants will be attending the marathon by invitation, we will only be able to offer a limited number of places for open registration. Early registration will therefore not guarantee you a place. We apologise in advance for any disappointment this may cause.

The price of the weekend is 95€ for 3 days and 130€ for 4 days. This includes the tango weekend itself, 2/3 breakfasts, 2/3 dinners (depending how long you stay), and snacks/drinks. Everyone is welcome to bring something yummy to share, too!
