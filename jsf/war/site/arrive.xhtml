The closest city is Macon (see link). The marathon venue is marked as "La Croix" on our map. The full address is:
La Croix
01290 Saint-Andre-d'Huiriat

We would recommend coming by car, as the place is fairly remote. Saint-Andre-d'Huiriat is 70km from Lyon, 150km from Geneva, 400km from Paris, and 420km from Zuerich. We will help to organise carpooling. Please contact us if you need a lift to the marathon, or, alternatively, would be willing to offer someone a lift.

The nearest train station to the marathon is Pont-de-Veyle. We will organize a shuttle service from Pont-de-Veyle on Friday at 1pm, 2pm, 3pm, 4pm, 5pm and 7pm. You can reach the station of Pont-de-Veyle via Macon-Ville or via Bourg-en-Bresse. Please note that Macon has two stations: Macon-Ville and Macon-Loche TGV. From Macon-Loche TGV, you have to take a bus to Macon-Ville, followed by a 5-min train ride to Pont-de-Veyle. Otherwise, a taxi from Macon-Loche TGV directly to the marathon will cost about 40€.

The nearest airports are Lyon (80km) and Geneva (150km). From there, you take a bus/train to Lyon-Centre or Bourg-en-Bresse, and can then travel by car or train to Pont-de-Veyle. The Paris airports are about 400km away, but the trip from Paris to the TGV station Macon-Loche takes only 1 hour 45 minutes.
